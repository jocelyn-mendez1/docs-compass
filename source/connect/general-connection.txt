General Connection
==================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page

   :depth: 1

The General Connection tab of the Compass Advanced Connection Options allows 
you to select a Connection String Scheme, enter your Hostname, and 
to choose to make your connection a Direct Connection.
